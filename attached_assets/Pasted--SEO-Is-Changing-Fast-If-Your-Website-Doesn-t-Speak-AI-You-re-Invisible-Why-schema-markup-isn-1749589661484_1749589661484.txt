🧠 Enter: Schema Markup Optimization

Schema markup is invisible code that tells search engines and AI tools exactly what your page is about.

With the right markup, you’re telling Google (and AI aggregators):

“This is a service page for local flooring installs.”
“This person is a verified dentist with 5-star reviews.”
“This blog post is an FAQ about solar panel tax credits.”
Without it, you're just hoping someone scrolls far enough to find what you offer.
