Build a high-converting, modern website for a professional AI consulting firm called Fripse. This site should position us as a premium solution for small business AI transformation, especially in industries like construction, estimating, and project management.

Goals:
	•	Establish trust and authority as a leading AI consulting firm
	•	Generate leads via clear, action-oriented CTAs
	•	Get users to book a free AI strategy call or sign up for our email list

Site Structure & Content

1. Hero Section (Above the Fold)
	•	Headline: “Smarter Work Starts Here.”
	•	Subtext: “Fripse helps small businesses save time and scale faster with tailored AI systems for operations, project management, and client communications.”
	•	Primary CTA: “Book a Free AI Strategy Call” (button, links to Calendly or form)
	•	Secondary CTA: “Not ready yet? Join our newsletter to see what’s possible.” (email capture)

2. Trust & Benefits Section
	•	Icons or tiles for key benefits:
	•	Save 10+ hours per week
	•	Reduce manual admin tasks by 80%
	•	Launch custom AI tools without writing code
	•	Headline: “AI That Pays for Itself”
	•	Add 1–2 short example use cases

3. Service Tiers Section
	•	Headline: “Choose Your AI Accelerator”
	•	Three visual columns:
	•	Quick Win Day – $2,500
	•	Audit + 2 automations
	•	Custom GPT prompt pack
	•	Training + support
	•	Ops Overhaul – $7,500 (highlight as “Most Popular”)
	•	Full system audit
	•	5–7 tailored AI tools
	•	SOPs, training, 30-day support
	•	AI Co-Pilot – $60,000
	•	2-week full install
	•	Staff training, tools, coaching
	•	60-day rollout support

4. What We Automate Section
	•	Short, benefit-led tiles:
	•	Project updates via auto-generated emails
	•	Meeting summaries from recordings
	•	Sales proposals from voice notes
	•	SOPs and training docs from raw input
	•	Add subtle animation or hover effects

5. Lead Magnet / Newsletter Signup
	•	Headline: “Steal Our Playbook”
	•	Subtext: “Get our free PDF: 5 AI Workflows Every Small Business Should Steal”
	•	Email capture form
	•	Setup for ConvertKit, Mailchimp, or Replit placeholder

6. Contact / CTA Section
	•	Headline: “Let’s Build Your AI Plan”
	•	Short copy: “Tell us about your business. We’ll show you what’s possible with AI in just one Zoom call.”
	•	Contact form with fields:
	•	Name
	•	Company
	•	Email
	•	Quick description of goals or problems
	•	Submit button labeled “Get My Free Consult”

Design Instructions
	•	Clean, minimalist white layout
	•	Use a highlight color (deep blue or emerald green)
	•	Modern font (Inter, Lato, or similar)
	•	Fully mobile responsive
	•	Sticky nav with anchor links to each section
	•	Scroll-trigger animations optional

Tech Specs
	•	Code in HTML/CSS/JavaScript (React optional)
	•	Include comments or markdown blocks for easy edits
	•	Add placeholder images/icons I can swap
	•	Include meta tags and title for SEO
	•	Include alt text for all images

Final note: this is a professional website for a business that wants to appear like a 5–10 person boutique AI firm—not a solo freelancer.
